Barcelona

Barcelona is the city capital of the entire country Spain, and is Spain's second most populated city with a population of 1.6 million. Barcelona is also the seventh-most populous urban area in the European Union.

Barcelona is one of the world's leading tourist, economic, trade fair, and cultural center, and influence in commerce. It is a major cultural, economic, and a financial center.
